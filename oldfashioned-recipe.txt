Ingredients
2 teaspoons simple syrup / sugar cube
1 teaspoon water
2 dashes bitters
1 cup ice cubes
1 (1.5 fl oz) jigger bourbon whiskey
1 slice orange
1 maraschino cherry

Directions
Prep time: 10min
Ready in : 10min

Pour the simple syrup, water, and bitters into a whiskey glass. Stir to combine, 
then place the ice cubes in the glass. Pour bourbon over the ice and garnish 
with the orange slice and maraschino cherry.
